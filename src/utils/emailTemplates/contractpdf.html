Prohibited Activities The Resident undertakes: No use, possession, or storage of illegal substances, including but not limited to drugs, narcotics, contraband, or banned items under the laws of Gujarat and India. Violation of this clause shall lead to immediate termination of the Agreement and reporting to the concerned authorities. 12. Visitors Policy No male visitors are allowed on the premises, irrespective of timing. Female friends/visitors may be permitted only in designated common areas and with prior intimation to the management. Overnight stay of any visitor is strictly prohibited. 13. Respect towards fellow residents, staff, and management is mandatory. 15. Safety & Security Entry and exit must be recorded as per the facility’s monitoring system (biometric, facial recognition, or manual logbook).
